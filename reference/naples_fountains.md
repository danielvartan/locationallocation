# Water fountains in the city of Naples, Italy

A [`sf`](https://r-spatial.github.io/sf/reference/st_as_sf.html) polygon
geometry object representing the locations of water fountains in the
city of Naples, Italy.

## Usage

``` r
naples_fountains
```
